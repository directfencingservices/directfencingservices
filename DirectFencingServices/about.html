About Direct Fencing & Services

Locally owned and operated in Kissimmee, Florida

Your Local Fence Installation Partner

Direct Fencing & Services is a locally owned and operated fence company serving Kissimmee, BVL, and the surrounding Central Florida area. We understand that your property is important to you, and we treat every project with the care and attention it deserves.

Unlike larger companies that may treat you like just another number, we take a hands-on approach to every fence installation. From your first call to the final walkthrough, you'll work directly with people who care about getting the job done right.

Honesty

We provide straightforward quotes with no hidden fees. If something changes during the project, we communicate immediately.

Transparency

We explain our process, material choices, and timeline clearly so you know exactly what to expect.

Code Compliance

We ensure every fence meets local building codes and HOA requirements. No shortcuts.

Communication

We keep you informed throughout the project. Questions? We're always available to answer them.

How We Work

Our process is designed to make your fence installation as smooth and stress-free as possible.

1

Project Management

We coordinate all aspects of your fence project from start to finish. You have a single point of contact who manages scheduling, materials, and communication.

2

Permit Handling

What Sets Us Apart

Experienced Crews

We work with skilled installation professionals who have years of experience in fence construction. They understand the challenges of Florida terrain and weather, and they know how to build fences that last.

Quality Materials

Local Knowledge

As a local business, we understand Kissimmee's building codes, common HOA requirements, and the specific challenges of fencing in Central Florida. This knowledge helps us anticipate issues before they become problems.

Customer-First Approach
