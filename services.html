Our Fencing Services

Quality fence installations for residential and light commercial properties throughout Central Florida

Wood Fencing

Wood fencing remains one of the most popular choices for Central Florida homeowners. Our wood fences offer timeless appeal, excellent privacy, and the flexibility to match any property style.

Fence Styles

  • Privacy fence (solid board) for maximum seclusion
  • Shadowbox style for airflow while maintaining privacy
  • Board-on-board for a finished look on both sides
  • Custom layouts to match your property's unique needs

Heights & Options

  • Available in 4ft, 6ft, and 8ft heights
  • Matching walk gates and drive gates
  • Stain-ready for custom coloring
  • Natural wood tones from cedar and treated pine

Common Stain Options

Wood fences can be stained in a variety of tones including natural cedar, redwood, walnut, and weathered gray. Staining helps protect the wood from Florida's sun and humidity while enhancing its natural beauty.

Vinyl Fencing

Vinyl fencing offers the beauty of traditional fencing with virtually no maintenance. It won't rot, warp, or require painting, making it an excellent long-term investment for Florida properties.

Benefits

  • Low maintenance - no painting, staining, or sealing required
  • Long-lasting durability against Florida weather
  • Resistant to rot, insects, and moisture damage
  • Easy to clean with just soap and water
  • Consistent color throughout - no fading

Standard Colors

  • White - classic, clean appearance
  • Tan/Almond - warm, natural look
  • Gray - modern, contemporary style

Style Options

We install vinyl privacy fences, picket styles, and semi-privacy designs. Vinyl is available in various heights and can include decorative lattice tops or post caps to complement your home's architecture.

Durafence (Steel Fencing)

Durafence is a commercial-grade steel fencing solution designed for maximum security and durability. It's an excellent choice for properties requiring strong perimeter protection without compromising on appearance.

Key Features

  • Heavy-duty steel construction for superior strength
  • Ideal for security-focused residential and commercial applications
  • Withstands Florida's harsh weather conditions
  • Long service life with minimal maintenance
  • Clean, professional appearance

Finish Options

  • Black powder-coated finish - durable and attractive
  • Galvanized steel - maximum corrosion resistance

Ideal Applications

Durafence steel fencing is perfect for perimeter security, commercial properties, equipment enclosures, and homeowners who want the strongest fence available. The powder-coated finish provides a clean look while resisting rust and wear.

Aluminum Picket Fencing

Aluminum fencing combines elegance with practicality. It's the go-to choice for pool enclosures in Florida, meeting safety codes while adding a decorative element to your property.

Benefits

  • Pool-safe and code-compliant designs available
  • Rust-resistant aluminum construction
  • Decorative appearance enhances property value
  • Low maintenance - no painting required
  • Available in multiple heights: 4ft, 5ft, and 6ft

Finish Colors

  • Black - timeless, elegant appearance
  • Bronze - warm, traditional look
  • White - clean, classic style

Gate Options

We install self-closing, self-latching gates that meet Florida pool safety requirements. Single swing and double swing gate configurations are available to match your access needs.

Request an Aluminum Fence Estimate

Chain Link Fencing

Chain link remains one of the most economical and durable fencing options available. It's perfect for defining property boundaries, containing pets, and securing areas without blocking visibility.

Advantages

  • Most cost-effective fencing option
  • Extremely durable and long-lasting
  • Allows visibility and airflow
  • Quick installation compared to other fence types
  • Easy to repair if damaged

Material Options

  • Galvanized steel - traditional silver appearance
  • Black vinyl-coated - softer look, added protection
  • Green vinyl-coated - blends with landscaping

Heights & Gates

Chain link fencing is available from 4ft to 6ft heights. We install walk gates and drive gates to match, including options for rolling gates on wider openings.
